Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about appointments and our services.

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Therapy can be beneficial for anyone experiencing emotional distress, life transitions, relationship challenges, or simply wanting to understand themselves better.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is typically an assessment where we'll discuss your concerns, goals, and history to develop a personalized treatment plan.

How long does therapy typically take?

The length of therapy varies depending on your individual needs and goals. We'll regularly check in about your progress and adjust as needed.

Can I switch between remote and in-person sessions?

Yes, we offer flexibility in session formats. You can choose remote or in-person sessions based on your preference and comfort level.
