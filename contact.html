Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about appointments and our services.

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

Therapy can be beneficial for anyone experiencing emotional distress, life transitions, relationship challenges, or simply wanting to understand themselves better. If you're feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or curious about personal growth, therapy can provide valuable support and insights.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is typically an assessment where we'll discuss your concerns, goals, and history. This helps us understand your unique situation and develop a personalized treatment plan. We'll also answer any questions you have about the therapy process.

How long does therapy typically take?

The length of therapy varies depending on your individual needs and goals. Some people see improvement in a few sessions, while others benefit from longer-term work. We'll regularly check in about your progress and adjust the treatment plan as needed.

Can I switch between remote and in-person sessions?

Yes, we offer flexibility in session formats. You can choose remote or in-person sessions based on your preference, schedule, and comfort level. Some clients find a combination works best for their needs.
